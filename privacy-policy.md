# OrbitCam Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

OrbitCam is a local-first dual-camera recording app for creators. We designed it so your videos stay on your device unless you choose to save them to Photos.

## Information We Collect

OrbitCam does not create accounts, collect analytics, show ads, or track you across apps and websites.

The app may access:

- Camera, when you record front and back camera video.
- Microphone, when you record audio with a video.
- Photo library add-only access, when you choose to save videos or export packs to Photos.
- Purchase status, through Apple's StoreKit, so the app can unlock Pro features you buy.

## Videos and Media

Videos are processed locally on your device. OrbitCam does not upload your videos to our servers. When you save to Photos, that action is initiated and controlled by you.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. OrbitCam receives purchase status from StoreKit, but we do not receive or store your payment card details.

## Support

If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or a description of the issue.

## Children

OrbitCam is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes
