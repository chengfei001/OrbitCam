# OrbitCam Support

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Thanks for using OrbitCam. If you need help, contact:

chengfei001@gmail.com

Please include:

- Your OrbitCam app version.
- Your iPhone model and iOS version.
- Whether the issue happened while recording, exporting, saving to Photos, or purchasing Pro.
- A short description of what you expected and what happened instead.

## Common Checks

- Camera or microphone unavailable: open iOS Settings, find OrbitCam, and allow Camera and Microphone.
- Save to Photos failed: make sure OrbitCam can add items to Photos and that your device has enough free storage.
- Pro purchase not visible: open OrbitCam again on the same Apple ID and try Restore Purchases from the paywall.
- Export pack failed: try exporting one format first, then confirm enough device storage is available.
